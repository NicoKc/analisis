    $ F(x,y,z) = x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = r^2 $

    QvQ $ \exists (x,y,z) \in \real^3 : \grad F(x,y,z) = 0$

    $ \grad F(x,y,z) = (2x, 2y, 2z) $

    $ \grad F(x,y,z) = (2x, 2y, 2z) = (0,0,0) \Leftrightarrow (x,y,z) = (0,0,0) $
